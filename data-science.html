  • Prediction on Professional Starcraft 2 Games.

    Starcraft is one of the most competitive PC games in the world. However, match records and game statistics can be really hard to obtain online. By building an intelligent web scrapper in Python with Beautiful Soup , professional Starcraft 2 players and premium match records are harvested and analyzed with help of Pandas. Predictions on ongoing/future tournaments are made.
