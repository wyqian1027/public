Exploration

  • How to Mix Watercolor Paints?

    Surprisingly, substractive color mixing is still a mystery to be solved completely. This app predicts resultant color after combining two watercolor paints, base on two most prevailing theories in color mixing: CMYK model and XYZ model. The CMYK model is predominately used in painting/printing industry, where Cyan, Magneta, Yellow and Black are used as primary colors. The XYZ model is a model of quantitative mapping between distributions of wavelengths in the electromagnetic visible spectrum and physiologically perceived colors in human color vision. Credits of specific parameters in XYZ model goes to Adobe@ RGB (1998) Color Image Encoding.
